About

Our project, “What Now?”, is an interactive scheduling assistant that can be used to easily and efficiently create and update the user’s schedule. The project serves as an effective time management tool that will improve one’s productivity and help them stay organized.

This website will aggregate weekly updates, the documentation, and our GANTT chart for easy access and quick updating.
